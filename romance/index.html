Grave Seasons Romance

Grave Seasons has 12 potential romance options. Some can be selected as the supernatural killer in a playthrough, so relationship scenes and consequences may vary with the active campaign and with who survives.

How relationships affect the story

Players complete quests and earn the trust of Ashenridge residents. Official descriptions say everything you do—or do not do—can affect how the story unfolds, and it is possible to romance the killer.
