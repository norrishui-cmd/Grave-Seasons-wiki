About GraveSeasons Wiki & Guide

GraveSeasons Wiki & Guide is an unofficial English resource built for players who want clear farming plans, character notes, romance routing, release updates, and spoiler-safe mystery tools for Grave Seasons.

What This Site Covers

The site organizes public pre-release information and launch-ready guide templates for crops, residents, gifts, platforms, suspects, and endings. After release, each page can be updated with verified in-game data.

Spoiler Policy

Mystery content should be structured in spoiler tiers. First-time players should see hints and tracking tools first, while full culprit, ending, and case-branch details should remain clearly marked.
