Grave Seasons Suspects

There is no single universal Grave Seasons culprit. Each playthrough selects from a pool of authored supernatural-killer campaigns, and every campaign has its own reasoning, kill style, and targets.

Suspect Template

  • Resident name, normal schedule, suspicious schedule changes, and last-seen notes.
  • Possible motive, known relationships, hidden conflicts, and item evidence.
  • Evidence strength, spoiler tier, verification status, and ending relevance.

Variable Killer Strategy

If the culprit changes between playthroughs, the guide should prioritize how to verify the killer in a specific save instead of only listing a universal answer.

Random selection versus random story

The active campaign can change between playthroughs, but the mystery is authored rather than procedurally assembled. A suspect guide must identify the relevant campaign evidence instead of ranking every resident with a generic probability score.

Spoiler-safe suspect rules

Resident hubs show ordinary roles and relationships only. Culprit confirmation, target lists, contradictions, and decisive evidence belong behind explicit spoilers on campaign pages and should never appear in titles or meta descriptions.
