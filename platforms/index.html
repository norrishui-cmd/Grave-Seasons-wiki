Platforms

Grave Seasons Platforms

Grave Seasons is officially announced for PC via Steam, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and Nintendo Switch. Xbox Game Pass availability has also been announced. The current publisher launch window is Fall 2026.

Confirmed platforms

  • PC via Steam
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox Series X|S
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Xbox Game Pass

Not currently announced

PS4, Xbox One, mobile, and Mac versions are not listed in current official storefront information. Cross-save, exact console performance, price, preload time, and install size also remain unconfirmed.

PC features and requirements

Steam lists single-player, Steam Achievements, and Family Sharing. Complete CPU, GPU, memory, storage, and operating-system requirements remain TBD; only a 64-bit processor and operating system are currently required.

Verification

Platform details were reviewed July 17, 2026 against the official publisher, Steam, and PlayStation listings. Store records that still show August 14 may not reflect the current Fall 2026 delay.
