Can You Romance the Killer in Grave Seasons?

Yes. Official descriptions confirm that a romance option can also be the active killer in a playthrough. That changes the route into a darker version rather than removing romance entirely.

Quick facts

  • Romancing a killer: confirmed
  • Killer identity can vary by campaign
  • Exact candidate list: not fully published

What the confirmed information means

Romance and killer state overlap. A character's ordinary romance route and killer-specific route can therefore contain different scenes, risks, and consequences.

What is still unknown

Exact dates, values, routes, penalties, rewards, and campaign-specific outcomes are excluded unless an official source or repeatable retail-build test supports them.
