Is the Grave Seasons Player an Escaped Convict?

Yes. The player character is an escaped convict who comes to Ashenridge hoping to live peacefully and take over a farm connected to a former cellmate.

Quick facts

  • Player premise: escaped convict
  • New home: Ashenridge farm
  • Name and appearance: customizable

What the confirmed information means

This fixed premise explains why the protagonist is new to town while still allowing the player's identity and appearance to be customized.
