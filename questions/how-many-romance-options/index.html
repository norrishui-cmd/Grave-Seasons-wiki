How Many Romance Options Are in Grave Seasons?

Grave Seasons has 12 potential romance options. The complete verified list and every route requirement have not yet been published on the official storefront.

Quick facts

  • Potential romance options: 12
  • Some may be selected as the killer
  • Route requirements: not yet published

What the confirmed information means

The number is confirmed by Steam, but names should only enter the romance database when their eligibility is directly revealed. A resident being named does not automatically make them romanceable.

What is still unknown

Exact dates, values, routes, penalties, rewards, and campaign-specific outcomes are excluded unless an official source or repeatable retail-build test supports them.
