How Many Items Are in Grave Seasons?

Grave Seasons advertises more than 600 collectible, discoverable, or craftable items. The final item list and category totals have not been released.

Quick facts

  • Items: 600+
  • Acquisition: collecting, discovery, and crafting
  • Complete list: not yet published

What the confirmed information means

The count supports a large item database, but it does not justify empty item URLs. Each item page needs a verified name plus a source, use, recipe, value, quest, or protection function.

What is still unknown

Exact dates, values, routes, penalties, rewards, and campaign-specific outcomes are excluded unless an official source or repeatable retail-build test supports them.

How this answer will be updated

After launch, this page will retain the direct answer and add tested steps, version notes, relevant platform differences, and links to the exact entity or walkthrough pages.

Sources and update policy

Current facts are checked against official publisher page and official Steam listing and official storefronts where relevant. Last reviewed: July 18, 2026. Community-compiled facts are limited to developer-revealed names, roles, relationships, and locations; unannounced gameplay details are labeled rather than inferred.
