Does Grave Seasons Have Character Customization?

Yes. Character customization is officially confirmed, and developer-revealed material indicates the player's appearance, name, and pronouns can be customized.

Quick facts

  • Character customization: confirmed
  • Name and pronouns: customizable
  • Complete option list: not published

What the confirmed information means

Customization is separate from the authored player premise: the protagonist arrives as an escaped convict seeking a new life on an Ashenridge farm.
