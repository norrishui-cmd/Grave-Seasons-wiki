What Kind of Game Is Grave Seasons?

Grave Seasons is a single-player isometric pixel-art farming and life simulation built around an authored supernatural murder mystery.

Quick facts

  • Genres: farming sim, life sim, RPG, mystery
  • Presentation: isometric pixel art
  • Mode: single-player

What the confirmed information means

The cozy and horror systems are intertwined: farming and crafting provide resources, relationships reveal residents, and investigation can help protect potential victims.
