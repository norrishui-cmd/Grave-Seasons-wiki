Does a Resident Death End the Story in Grave Seasons?

No. Official descriptions say character deaths unlock different stories, while survival can reveal other storylines. A death changes the campaign rather than automatically ending it.

Quick facts

  • Death can unlock stories
  • Survival can unlock alternate stories
  • Consequences persist through the year

What the confirmed information means

Victim protection matters, but the narrative is designed to continue through outcomes. Guides should document both survival and death branches with clear spoilers.
