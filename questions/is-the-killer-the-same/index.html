Player question

Is the Killer the Same in Every Grave Seasons Playthrough?

No. Each playthrough selects a potential supernatural killer from a pool of candidates. Each candidate has an authored campaign with their own motive, method, and targets.

Quick facts

  • Same killer every year: no
  • Campaigns: authored
  • Targets and methods can differ

What the confirmed information means

The changing culprit is a replay system, not a single mystery with one permanent answer. Suspect guides must identify the campaign instead of declaring one universal killer.

What is still unknown

Exact dates, values, routes, penalties, rewards, and campaign-specific outcomes are excluded unless an official source or repeatable retail-build test supports them.
