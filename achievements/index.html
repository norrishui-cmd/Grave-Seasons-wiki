Achievements

Grave Seasons Achievements

Grave Seasons will support Steam Achievements. The complete achievement names, hidden flags, requirements, trophy equivalents, and missable conditions have not yet been published.

Achievement Checklist Fields

  • Achievement name, unlock condition, hidden status, spoiler level, and missable status.
  • Related ending, related romance route, collection requirement, or mystery branch.
  • Safe hint, exact method, and recommended save/checkpoint strategy.

What is confirmed?

The official Steam feature list includes Steam Achievements. That confirms PC achievement support, but it does not reveal the list or confirm whether PlayStation trophies and Xbox achievements will use identical requirements. Platform-specific lists must be checked separately.

How to use this page before launch

Bookmark this hub for the verified launch checklist; every entry will separate a spoiler-free hint from the exact unlock method.

Completion-guide quality rule

An achievement receives a dedicated URL only when its real name and unlock condition are visible in an official list or verified retail build. Guessed milestones, invented hidden achievements, and placeholder trophy pages remain outside the sitemap.
