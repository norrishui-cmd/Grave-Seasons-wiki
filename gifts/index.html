Characters Romance Crops

How are relationships confirmed to progress?

Players complete quests for Ashenridge residents and build their trust. Everything the player does—or does not do—can affect the story, and twelve potential romance options are confirmed. That does not by itself confirm specific loved or hated items.

Gift-page quality rule

A gift item or character preference receives an indexable URL only after the item name, preference reaction, and item source are verified. The site will not convert generic character interests into invented loved-gift lists.
