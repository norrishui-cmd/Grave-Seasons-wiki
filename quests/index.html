Grave Seasons Quests

Grave Seasons includes resident quests and investigation quests. Developer interview material explains that discovered clues enter the journal and can become trackable tasks that direct the player toward a related person or place.

Quest Fields

  • Quest giver, trigger date, location, prerequisites, required items, and reward.
  • Deadline, missable status, related resident, related ending, and spoiler level.
  • Step-by-step solution with a spoiler-free summary first.

How investigation quests begin

Finding a clue can add it to the journal and generate a follow-up lead. The journal tracks what was found, but the player still needs town knowledge—for example, knowing who works at a business and where that person goes when the building is closed.

Walkthrough publication rule

A quest page needs a verified trigger, objective, required steps, relevant location or resident, reward or outcome, failure conditions, and spoiler label. Quest names without a concrete solution stay out of the sitemap.
