Grave Seasons Crowbar | Break-Ins and Secret Areas

The player carries a crowbar that can force certain locked doors and open access to secret areas, but getting caught while entering residents’ homes can have consequences.

Confirmed data

  • Item type: exploration and break-in tool
  • Confirmed use: forcing locked doors
  • Access role: reaching secret areas
  • Detection risk: confirmed
  • Exact acquisition and upgrade path: not published

What the crowbar does

Official gameplay material shows the escaped-convict protagonist carrying a crowbar and using it to force entry through locked doors. This connects a named tool to the broader break-and-enter mechanic instead of treating every locked entrance as scenery.

How it supports investigations

Residents keep clues, histories, and secrets behind private spaces. A forced entrance may reveal evidence or a hidden area, but the correct building, timing, and campaign relevance can change. The crowbar is therefore an access tool, not a guaranteed solution to every murder campaign.

What still needs launch verification

The game has not published the crowbar’s acquisition point, stamina cost, durability, upgrade tree, noise radius, or every compatible door. Those values will be added only after repeatable testing in the retail build.
