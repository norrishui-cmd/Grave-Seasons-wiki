Grave Seasons Kitchen Crafting | Recipe Uses

The farm kitchen is one of two confirmed crafting locations. It processes recipes separately from the shed, although the full recipe list and station rules are not yet public.

Confirmed data

  • Crafting station: farm kitchen
  • Recipe list: not yet published
  • Inputs may include crops, forage and fish
  • Mystery connection: possible through crafted resources
  • Separate shed crafting: confirmed

Confirmed kitchen role

Steam’s official description identifies the kitchen as a place where the player crafts recipes. Farming, fishing, mining, and foraging all supply ingredients, so kitchen records will eventually connect every verified input to its season, source, result, and practical use.

Kitchen versus shed

The official wording names both the kitchen and shed, which indicates distinct farm crafting locations. It does not yet establish which recipe categories belong to each station, whether either station requires an upgrade, or whether food provides stamina or other effects.

Database quality boundary

Individual recipe URLs will not be generated from guessed names. A recipe needs a real name plus ingredients, station, unlock condition, and a useful outcome before it qualifies for the sitemap.

Source and verification boundary

This page uses the official Steam game description. Counts and announced functions are reported as confirmed; unpublished names, prices, schedules, recipes, outcomes, and balance values are not inferred.
