Grave Seasons Shed Crafting | Tools and Occult Items

The shed is a confirmed farm crafting location. Exact recipes remain unpublished, but official descriptions place it alongside kitchen crafting and the game’s protective occult-item system.

Confirmed data

  • Crafting station: farm shed
  • Exact recipes: not published
  • Occult crafting: confirmed elsewhere in the farm system
  • Tool and building upgrades: confirmed
  • Recipe requirements: awaiting retail verification

Confirmed shed function

The official Steam overview explicitly says recipes can be crafted in the kitchen or shed. That makes the shed a functional station rather than a decorative farm building and gives future recipe pages a verified station field.

Connection to protection and upgrades

Farm resources can feed ordinary production as well as mysterious objects used against supernatural threats. Tools and farm buildings can also be upgraded, but current material does not prove that every occult object is made in the shed or that the shed itself has upgrade tiers.

What the full guide will test

Launch testing will record station access, recipe unlocks, ingredients, craft time, result quantity, campaign relevance, and building prerequisites. Unverified recipe names will remain excluded.

Source and verification boundary

This page uses the official Steam game description. Counts and announced functions are reported as confirmed; unpublished names, prices, schedules, recipes, outcomes, and balance values are not inferred.
