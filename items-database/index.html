Frequently asked questions

Item FAQ

What does the 600-plus item count include?

Steam says players can collect, discover, or craft more than 600 items, but it does not publish a category breakdown.

How are items obtained in Grave Seasons?

Official copy identifies three broad methods: collecting, discovering, and crafting more than 600 items.

Can occult items save residents?

Some mysterious crafted items can help protect potential victims from supernatural killers.

Where are recipes crafted?

Official descriptions name two farm crafting locations: the kitchen and the shed.

Can tools be upgraded in Grave Seasons?

Yes. Tool upgrades are an officially confirmed part of farm progression.

Can farm buildings be upgraded?

Yes. Steam confirms upgrades for buildings on the player's farm.

Can fishing and mining ingredients help solve mysteries?

Yes. Official copy connects new ingredients found through fishing and mining with unraveling Ashenridge's mysteries.

Named tools and crafting stations

Crowbar | Break-Ins and Secret Areas

The player carries a crowbar that can force certain locked doors and open access to secret areas, but getting caught while entering residents’ homes can have consequences.

Kitchen Crafting | Recipe Uses

The farm kitchen is one of two confirmed crafting locations. It processes recipes separately from the shed, although the full recipe list and station rules are not yet public.

Shed Crafting | Tools and Occult Items

The shed is a confirmed farm crafting location. Exact recipes remain unpublished, but official descriptions place it alongside kitchen crafting and the game’s protective occult-item system.
