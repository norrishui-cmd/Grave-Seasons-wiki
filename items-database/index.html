Items

Grave Seasons Items Database

Official Steam text references 600+ items to collect or craft. That scale needs a dedicated items database URL with filters and clear field standards, not only a broad resources page.

Item Fields

  • Name, category, source, season, location, sell value, recipe use, gift use, quest use, and upgrade use.
  • Collection status, spoiler level, occult relevance, and whether the item is found, bought, crafted, mined, fished, or foraged.
  • Related pages for recipes, resources, gifts, quests, and achievements.

Database Growth Plan

Before launch this page defines the schema. After launch it can become the parent for individual item pages and filterable tables while preventing thin one-line item pages.

600+ does not mean 600 empty pages

The official listing confirms more than 600 items can be collected, discovered, or crafted. Each item URL still requires a verified name, source, and meaningful use or value. Unknown records, guessed slugs, and token-swapped descriptions remain outside the sitemap until evidence is available.
