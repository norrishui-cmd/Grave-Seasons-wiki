Recipes

Grave Seasons Recipes Guide

Official feature descriptions mention recipes in the kitchen and shed. Recipes therefore sit between farming, resource gathering, gifts, quests, and supernatural protection.

Recipe Fields

  • Recipe name, station, ingredients, unlock source, energy or effect, sell value, gift use, and quest use.
  • Whether the recipe belongs to cooking, crafting, farm upgrades, occult preparation, or collection completion.
  • Ingredient source links to crops, fishing, mining, foraging, shops, and resources.

Content Quality Guardrail

Individual recipe pages should only be created when there is enough verified data: ingredients, unlock method, use cases, related quests, and routing advice.

Recipe indexing rule

Kitchen and shed recipes are confirmed, including mysterious protective items. A recipe gets its own indexable page only after its ingredients, station, unlock condition, result, and at least one useful effect are known. Placeholder recipes and combinatorial ingredient pages are excluded from the sitemap.
