Foraging

Grave Seasons Foraging Guide

Foraging is part of the official activity mix and naturally connects the farm, forest, mountainside, coast, recipes, gifts, and mystery items. A dedicated page helps capture seasonal item searches after launch.

Forage Table Fields

  • Item name, season, area, respawn pattern, sell value, recipe use, gift use, and quest use.
  • Whether the item appears in forest, coast, mountainside, farm edge, town districts, or other Ashenridge routes.
  • Occult relevance and spoiler status if a wild item becomes part of victim protection or clue verification.

Internal Link Plan

Foraged items should connect to recipes, resources, items database, gifts, and map pages. This creates useful paths for both cozy farming players and mystery-focused players.

Foraging evidence standard

Foraging and uncovering pieces of Ashenridge history are confirmed activities. Individual wild-item pages need a verified name, location or season, and at least one recipe, quest, sale, gift, crafting, or investigation use. Preview scenery alone is not enough to invent an item or publish a location table.
