Grave Seasons Secret Areas and Locked Doors

Secret areas are confirmed in Grave Seasons, and some locked doors can be forced with the player’s crowbar. Access can expose clues, but entering private spaces risks being caught.

Confirmed data

  • Secret areas: confirmed
  • Locked-door access: crowbar
  • Private homes: enterable
  • Detection consequence: confirmed
  • Universal access route: not confirmed

How secret access works

Gameplay explanations show the player using a crowbar to open locked doors and reach otherwise hidden spaces. Steam also confirms breaking into local homes, with an explicit warning not to get caught.

Why timing matters

Ashenridge follows a day-and-night schedule and residents are connected to businesses and homes. A useful access guide must therefore track the relevant building, time, resident presence, door state, and active killer campaign rather than promise that one route always works.

Spoiler and evidence policy

Pages for specific secret rooms will hide killer, victim, and ending information behind warnings. A room only receives its own URL after its real location and at least one clue, item, quest, character, or route connection are verified.

Source and verification boundary

This page uses the official Perfect Garbage Steam update. Counts and announced functions are reported as confirmed; unpublished names, prices, schedules, recipes, outcomes, and balance values are not inferred.
