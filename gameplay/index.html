Grave Seasons Gameplay Guide

Grave Seasons combines seasonal farming and town life with an authored supernatural murder mystery. Farming, crafting, quests, relationships, investigation, victim protection, choices, and replay campaigns are connected systems.

Quick facts

  • Genre: narrative farming and life simulation
  • Story: supernatural murder mystery
  • Residents: 30+
  • Romance options: 12
  • Items: 600+
  • Campaign structure: one in-game year

Confirmed gameplay systems

Farming

Grave Seasons includes seasonal crops grown on different types of farm plots. Harvests support the town economy and also play a role in solving Ashenridge's mysteries.

Character Customization

Player-character customization is confirmed, and the farm includes potential aesthetic choices. The complete option list has not been shown.

Autosave and Choices

The game checks and autosaves decisions to shape the story. Character deaths and survival unlock different storylines, so choices can carry lasting consequences.

Replayability

Each new year can select a supernatural killer from a pool of candidates, with authored reasoning, kill styles, and targets. Previous work is also tracked across replays.

Grave Seasons Journal and Clue Tracking

Grave Seasons has a journal system that records clues found during investigations. Those clues can become trackable investigation quests, so players do not need to memorize every discovery.

Are Grave Seasons Killers Random?

Grave Seasons does not procedurally invent a murderer. A playthrough selects from a pool of authored supernatural-killer campaigns, each with its own reasoning, kill style, and targets.

Can Characters Die in Grave Seasons?

Yes. Ashenridge residents can die, and official descriptions say their death or survival unlocks different storylines. The player can try to prevent murders but is also allowed to let chaos unfold.

Can You Save Everyone in Grave Seasons?

Grave Seasons lets players investigate danger and craft items that may protect potential victims, but no official source currently guarantees that every resident can be saved in one playthrough.

the One-Year Calendar

A Grave Seasons playthrough covers one in-game year with changing seasons. The finite year creates time pressure for farming, relationships, investigation, and protecting residents.
