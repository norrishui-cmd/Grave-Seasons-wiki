Grave Seasons 24-Hour Schedule Explained

Grave Seasons runs on a 24-hour schedule and does not force the player to stop exploring when night begins. Players can stay awake beyond a normal day and experience a darker side of Ashenridge.

Confirmed data

  • Schedule: 24 hours
  • Forced nighttime stop: no
  • 24+ hours awake: supported
  • Night exploration: confirmed
  • Sleep and exhaustion penalties: not yet published

No hard nighttime cutoff

Perfect Garbage says it avoided a hard line that forces rest at night. This lets the town and its residents show different facets after dark and allows investigation to continue beyond the daytime farming routine.

Night changes exploration

The developer specifically connects darkness with ominous moments and searching through neighbors’ belongings. Resident routines, building access, visibility, danger, and potential clues can therefore differ by time of day.

Still-unpublished clock rules

The real-time length of an in-game hour, pause behavior, sleep benefits, exhaustion, fainting, recovery costs, and event cutoffs remain unconfirmed. Those mechanics require retail-build testing.
