Grave Seasons Character Customization Options

At launch, character customization is planned to include 35 unique wearables, 25 skin tones, 18 hairstyles with 33 color choices, and a mix-and-match clothing system with 14 color options.

Confirmed data

  • Unique wearables: 35
  • Skin tones: 25
  • Hairstyles: 18
  • Hair-color choices: 33
  • Clothing color options: 14
  • Animation: hand-animated

Confirmed customization counts

Perfect Garbage published exact launch-facing counts in its June development update. These figures are more useful than a generic customization claim because they define the announced size of the visual creator without inventing individual clothing names.

Mix-and-match clothing

The developer describes a mix-and-match clothing setup with 14 colorful options. The wording confirms flexible appearance combinations, but it does not yet publish clothing slots, unlock costs, shop inventory, body types, or whether every color applies to every wearable.

What will become separate pages

Named wearables should receive individual pages only when their appearance, source, price, slot, color support, or unlock condition can be verified. The 35-item count alone will not be expanded into empty records.

Source and verification boundary

This page uses the official Perfect Garbage Steam update. Counts and announced functions are reported as confirmed; unpublished names, prices, schedules, recipes, outcomes, and balance values are not inferred.
