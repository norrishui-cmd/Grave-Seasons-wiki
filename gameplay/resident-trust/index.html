Grave Seasons Resident Trust and Town Quests

Players build trust by completing quests for Ashenridge residents. That trust helps reveal more about townsfolk and can affect how the murder investigation and story unfold.

Confirmed data

  • Trust system: confirmed
  • Primary method: resident quests
  • Story consequences: confirmed
  • Investigation relevance: confirmed
  • Exact values and thresholds: not published

How trust is earned

Official feature material says the player completes quests for townsfolk and builds their trust. This creates a direct link between daily-life tasks and the central investigation: learning about a resident can clarify motives, routines, relationships, and hidden information.

Trust is not only a romance meter

Grave Seasons allows romance, but trust supports the broader community and mystery systems. A useful guide should separate normal resident trust, romance progression, quest outcomes, and campaign-specific evidence rather than collapse them into one affection score.

What remains unknown

The interface, numerical thresholds, decay rules, failed-quest penalties, gift contribution, and exact dialogue gates have not been published. Character guides will add those details only when observed in an official build.

This page uses the official Steam game description. Counts and announced functions are reported as confirmed; unpublished names, prices, schedules, recipes, outcomes, and balance values are not inferred.
