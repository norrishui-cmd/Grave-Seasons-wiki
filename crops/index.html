Farming

Grave Seasons Crops

Seasonal crop farming is a confirmed Grave Seasons system. Players grow crops on different types of farm plots and sell their harvest in Ashenridge, while harvested resources can also contribute to unraveling the town's mysteries.

What is known before release?

Changing seasons affect the world, and the farm supports tool and building upgrades plus aesthetic customization. Official sources have not yet published crop names, seed prices, growth days, regrowth, sale values, or processing recipes.
