Release News · July 19, 2026

Grave Seasons Launch Window Moves to Fall 2026

Blumhouse Games now lists Grave Seasons for Fall 2026, replacing the previously announced August 14 date.

At a glance

  • Topic: Release Date
  • Status: confirmed pre-release information
  • Published: July 19, 2026
  • Primary reference: official publisher page

What the new information establishes

The publisher's live game page uses the wording “Launching Fall 2026,” while Steam currently shows the broader year 2026. No replacement day, regional unlock time, price, or preload schedule has been announced. Older articles and cached store records may still display August 14.

What this means for players and our guides

Players should treat Fall 2026 as the current window and avoid countdowns tied to the former date. Our release page will update only when Perfect Garbage, Blumhouse Games, or an official storefront publishes a new day. Wishlist notifications remain the safest way to follow availability.

Source and verification boundary

This update is based on the official publisher page. We distinguish a confirmed feature or count from details that have not been published. Exact values, routes, schedules, rewards, culprits, and platform behavior are added only after an official update or repeatable retail-build verification.
