Grave Seasons Remains Planned for Xbox Game Pass

Grave Seasons has been presented as an Xbox Game Pass title alongside its Xbox Series X|S and PC release plans.

At a glance

  • Topic: Release Date
  • Status: confirmed pre-release information
  • Published: July 19, 2026
  • Primary reference: official publisher page

What the new information establishes

The release delay means the exact subscription unlock date also remains unsettled. Current public material supports planned Game Pass availability, but it does not provide a final regional rollout, subscription tier, preload date, or separate confirmation for every PC and console catalog.

What this means for players and our guides

Players should recheck the final Xbox store entry when the replacement date is announced. Our Game Pass page will distinguish console and PC availability only when Microsoft publishes those labels, rather than assuming that every Game Pass announcement covers all tiers and regions.

Source and verification boundary

This update is based on the official publisher page. We distinguish a confirmed feature or count from details that have not been published. Exact values, routes, schedules, rewards, culprits, and platform behavior are added only after an official update or repeatable retail-build verification.
