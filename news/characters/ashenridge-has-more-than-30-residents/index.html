Ashenridge Will Have More Than 30 Residents

The official Steam description says players can get to know more than 30 residents across the town of Ashenridge.

At a glance

  • Topic: Characters
  • Status: confirmed pre-release information
  • Published: July 19, 2026
  • Primary reference: official Steam description

What the new information establishes

Residents are connected to jobs, businesses, homes, quests, friendships, romance, clues, and campaign outcomes. The active killer and victim list can change between playthroughs, while a resident's death or survival unlocks different story material.

What this means for players and our guides

Our roster will add an individual URL only when a real name and useful identity information are known. We will not generate anonymous Resident 1–30 pages or invent birthdays, gifts, schedules, quests, and romance eligibility to match the announced count.

Source and verification boundary

This update is based on the official Steam description. We distinguish a confirmed feature or count from details that have not been published. Exact values, routes, schedules, rewards, culprits, and platform behavior are added only after an official update or repeatable retail-build verification.
