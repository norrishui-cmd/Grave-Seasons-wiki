Farming Resources Feed the Grave Seasons Murder Mystery

Crops, foraging, fishing, mining, and crafting are connected to investigation and victim protection rather than serving only as an economy loop.

At a glance

  • Topic: Gameplay
  • Status: confirmed pre-release information
  • Published: July 19, 2026
  • Primary reference: official Steam description

What the new information establishes

Official descriptions say players can grow seasonal crops on different plot types, unearth pieces of Ashenridge history while farming and foraging, and discover ingredients through fishing and mining. Kitchen and shed recipes include mysterious occult items that may protect potential victims.

What this means for players and our guides

This connection means efficiency guides need more than sale-price rankings. Each crop or resource may have an economic use, recipe role, quest link, or campaign-specific protective value. Our database will not assign those values until a named item and repeatable use are visible in official material or the retail build.

Source and verification boundary

This update is based on the official Steam description. We distinguish a confirmed feature or count from details that have not been published. Exact values, routes, schedules, rewards, culprits, and platform behavior are added only after an official update or repeatable retail-build verification.
