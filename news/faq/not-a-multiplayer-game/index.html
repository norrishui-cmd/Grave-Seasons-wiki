Grave Seasons Is Currently a Single-Player Game

Steam lists Grave Seasons as single-player, and no online co-op, local co-op, competitive mode, or crossplay support has been announced.

At a glance

  • Topic: FAQ
  • Status: confirmed pre-release information
  • Published: July 19, 2026
  • Primary reference: official Steam feature list

What the new information establishes

The game's variety comes from authored killer campaigns, relationships, resident deaths and survival, autosaved choices, seasons, and tracked work from previous years. Official descriptions frame these systems around one player's story in Ashenridge.

What this means for players and our guides

We will not create setup or crossplay guides without a multiplayer announcement. If the feature list changes, platform-specific details will be verified before updating this answer.

Source and verification boundary

This update is based on the official Steam feature list. We distinguish a confirmed feature or count from details that have not been published. Exact values, routes, schedules, rewards, culprits, and platform behavior are added only after an official update or repeatable retail-build verification.
