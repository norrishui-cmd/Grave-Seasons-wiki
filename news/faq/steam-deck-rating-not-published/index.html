Grave Seasons Has No Published Steam Deck Rating Yet

The PC release is confirmed, but the public Steam listing does not currently provide a Verified or Playable compatibility result for Steam Deck.

What the new information establishes

A Windows Steam release alone does not establish handheld quality. Controller prompts, text size, video playback, frame pacing, battery use, launcher behavior, and sleep-resume support all affect compatibility.

What this means for players and our guides

We will use Valve's badge or repeatable final-build testing before recommending settings. Preview footage cannot substitute for a retail compatibility result that may change with launch patches.

Source and verification boundary

This update is based on the official Steam listing. We distinguish a confirmed feature or count from details that have not been published. Exact values, routes, schedules, rewards, culprits, and platform behavior are added only after an official update or repeatable retail-build verification.
