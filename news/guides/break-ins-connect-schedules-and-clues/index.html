Home Break-Ins Make Resident Schedules Part of Investigation

Breaking into residents' homes is a confirmed investigation mechanic, making location access and daily routines important parts of a clue guide.

At a glance

  • Topic: Guide Index
  • Status: confirmed pre-release information
  • Published: July 19, 2026
  • Primary reference: official developer page

What the new information establishes

Official material shows that the player can enter local homes while being warned not to get caught. This makes a break-in more than a fixed map instruction: useful guidance may depend on when a resident leaves, whether a door is accessible, what evidence belongs to a specific campaign, and what happens after detection.

What this means for players and our guides

Future walkthroughs will separate entry method, safe timing, searchable objects, evidence relevance, and relationship consequences. A page will identify whether information is universal or tied to a killer campaign. Until launch testing confirms tools and penalties, we will not publish guessed lockpicks, stealth statistics, or guaranteed safe hours.

Source and verification boundary

This update is based on the official developer page. We distinguish a confirmed feature or count from details that have not been published. Exact values, routes, schedules, rewards, culprits, and platform behavior are added only after an official update or repeatable retail-build verification.
