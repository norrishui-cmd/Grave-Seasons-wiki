Ashenridge Changes With Killer, Season, and Survival State

Official copy says the town changes depending on which killer is active, which residents are alive, and what season it is.

What the new information establishes

This means a single static schedule may not describe every playthrough. A shop can lose a worker, dialogue can change after a death, evidence can belong to one campaign, and seasonal resources or routes may create different reasons to revisit the same place.

What this means for players and our guides
