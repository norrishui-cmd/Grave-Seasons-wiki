Confirmed Game Loop

What You Will Do in Ashenridge

Official descriptions connect farm progress, relationships, and investigation. Exact optimal routes will be added only after they are tested in the retail build.

  1. Farm

    Grow seasonal crops on different plot types, fish, mine, forage, sell goods, and upgrade tools and farm buildings.

  2. Connect

    Meet more than 30 residents, complete quests to earn trust, and pursue one of 12 potential romance options.

  3. Investigate

    Search for clues, explore more than 70 interiors, and even break into homes while trying not to get caught.

  4. Protect

    Craft occult items, identify potential victims, and make lasting choices during the one-year campaign.
