Beginner Route

First Week Game Plan

Grave Seasons players will be balancing profit, stamina, social knowledge, and suspicious behavior. This guide starts with a pre-launch route structure that can be replaced with exact dates, schedules, and quest triggers once the game is released.

  1. Day 1

    Clear the farm, plant starter crops, and log every resident name and common location before spending gifts.

  2. Day 2-3

    Prioritize core locations such as the shop, clinic, tavern, and town square to map daily routines.

  3. Day 4-5

    Start recording odd dialogue, missing time, conflicting statements, and suspicious items in the tracker.

  4. Weekend

    Review cash flow, relationship targets, and suspect notes before choosing next week's focus.
