Beginner Guide

Grave Seasons Beginner Guide

This starter guide focuses on the opening rhythm players will search for first: what to do on day one, how to balance crops and social time, and how to track the mystery without spoiling the culprit.

First Day Priorities

  • Clear enough farm space for starter crops without spending all stamina.
  • Visit core town locations and record resident names, roles, and common routes.
  • Save gifts until preferences are known unless the game clearly teaches safe early options.
  • Start a simple clue log for odd dialogue, missing time, and suspicious items.
