Grave Seasons Killers and Victims

Official Steam copy says each playthrough selects a supernatural killer from a pool of candidates, and each killer has their own campaign, reasoning, kill styles, and targets. That makes this one of the most important wiki hubs.

Candidate Page Template

  • Resident profile, normal routine, relationship routes, known secrets, and possible motive.
  • Save-specific verification signs, suspicious events, target pattern, and evidence contradictions.
  • Victim protection opportunities, occult item requirements, deadlines, and consequences if the resident dies.
  • Separate safe hints from full-spoiler campaign details, target order, and ending consequences.

Why This Needs Its Own URL

Players will search separately for "who is the killer", "how to save victims", and "can romance characters be killers". A combined hub can capture those searches while still protecting first-time players with spoiler gates and neutral language.

Campaign accuracy rule

Each playthrough selects a potential supernatural killer from a pool of authored campaigns, each with its own reasoning, kill style, and targets. Killer and victim pages must therefore identify the relevant campaign and story state. A single fixed culprit list would be inaccurate and would also spoil the central mystery.
