Victim Protection

Grave Seasons Victim Protection Guide

Official descriptions frame the mystery around protecting potential victims over a year while a supernatural killer changes between playthroughs. A victim protection hub gives players a safe way to plan without immediately revealing every target.

Protection Checklist

  • Resident, possible risk window, last-known route, required clue, protective item, and verification status.
  • Calendar deadline, night activity, relationship or quest dependency, and related suspect branch.
  • Spoiler tier: safe warning, light-spoiler clue, or full target confirmation.

Why It Should Stay Separate

Victim protection searches are different from suspect searches. Some players want to save residents without knowing the killer. Keeping this URL separate lets the site serve those players cleanly.
