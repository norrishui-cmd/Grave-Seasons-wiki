Investigation

Grave Seasons Investigation Guide

Investigation is the system that separates Grave Seasons from a standard farming sim. Players need a guide that helps them reason through clues without immediately spoiling the active killer, victim order, or ending branch.

What This Hub Should Track

  • Clue source, date found, location, related resident, evidence strength, and whether the clue is safe, light-spoiler, or full-spoiler.
  • Break-in opportunities and risks, since official materials mention entering locals' homes without getting caught.
  • Day and night changes, including late-night activity, missing time, suspicious routines, and 24+ hour stamina planning.
  • Occult items and protective methods that may help save potential victims from the supernatural killer.

Spoiler-Safe Format

Each investigation entry should begin with a neutral hint, then a verification checklist, then an optional spoiler block. That format fits the official variable-killer premise: a page should teach players how to verify what is happening in their save, not pretend every playthrough has one fixed answer.
