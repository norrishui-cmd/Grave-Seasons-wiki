Grave Seasons: Ashenridge Commercial District

The Commercial District is one of Ashenridge's seven named areas. The game lets players participate in the local economy by selling goods in town, but store names, inventories, prices, and opening hours remain unconfirmed.

Quick facts

  • Official major area: yes
  • Part of Ashenridge: yes
  • Exact map and sublocations: not published
  • Primary guide uses: selling farm goods, shopping, upgrades, quests, and social routes

What is confirmed

Ashenridge Commercial District appears in the official list of seven major places to visit. Ashenridge has more than 70 interiors across the wider town, and locations can change with the active killer, resident survival, and season.
