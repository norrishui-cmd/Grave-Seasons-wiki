Grave Seasons Locations

Ashenridge has seven confirmed major areas: Your Farm, the Mountainside, the Forest, the Local District, the Commercial District, the Coast & Docks, and the Mines. Official copy also promises more than 70 interiors across the town.

Quick facts

  • Major areas: 7
  • Interiors: 70+
  • Residents: 30+
  • Location state can vary by season
  • Routes may change with killer and survival state

How the location database will grow

Each verified interior should eventually have one canonical page with access hours, residents, items, quests, clue relevance, season changes, and adjacent places. Empty combinations should never be indexed.

Why location state matters

The official description says Ashenridge changes depending on the active killer, who remains alive, and the season. Location pages therefore need state-specific notes instead of a single universal schedule.

Frequently asked questions

Location FAQ

What are the seven major areas in Grave Seasons?

The seven named areas are the Farm, Mountainside, Forest, Local District, Commercial District, Coast and Docks, and Mines.

Does Ashenridge really have more than 70 interiors?

Yes. The official Steam description states that Ashenridge contains more than 70 interior locations.

Is the player's farm one of the major areas?

Yes. Your Farm is explicitly included in the official list of seven major Ashenridge areas.

Where is the player farm located?

Official copy places the farm at the very top of Ashenridge's mountain.

Are the Coast and Docks one major area?

Yes. “The Coast & Docks” appears as one combined entry in the official seven-area list.

Are the Mines a major Ashenridge area?

Yes. The Mines are one of the seven official major locations and mining is a confirmed activity.

Can locations change between playthroughs?

Yes. Ashenridge can change with the active killer, resident survival, and season.
