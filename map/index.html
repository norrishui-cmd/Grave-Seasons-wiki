Grave Seasons Map

Ashenridge has seven confirmed major areas and more than 70 interior locations. The major areas are Your Farm, the Mountainside, the Forest, the Local District, the Commercial District, the Coast & Docks, and the Mines.

How the world can change

Official descriptions say places in Ashenridge can change according to the active killer, which residents remain alive, and the current season. A useful map therefore needs state notes instead of pretending one route is universal.
