Characters

Grave Seasons Characters

Grave Seasons has more than 30 residents in Ashenridge, including 12 potential romance options. Any potential killer can have an authored campaign with different reasoning, kill style, and targets, so a resident may play a different role across separate years.

What is confirmed about residents?

Residents live across more than 70 interiors and seven major areas. Players build trust by completing quests and can uncover hidden sides of townsfolk while they are a killer or a victim. Choices, deaths, and survival can unlock different storylines.

Frequently asked questions

Character FAQ

Do resident quests help build trust?

Yes. Official feature descriptions connect completing quests for townsfolk with earning their trust and learning more about them.

Can a romance candidate become the killer?

Yes. Steam confirms that some of the 12 potential romance options can be selected as the killer in a playthrough.

Can a romance candidate become a victim?

Yes. Official descriptions allow townsfolk to be victims while relationships and survival outcomes continue to shape the story.

Can the same character have different story roles?

Yes. A resident can have different relevance when they are alive, dead, a victim, or the active killer in separate playthroughs.

Does the surviving resident roster change Ashenridge?

Yes. Steam says Ashenridge changes depending on which residents are alive, along with the active killer and current season.

Do character jobs help with investigations?

Yes. The journal example shows that a business-related clue can require knowing which resident works there and where they spend time.

Are all Ashenridge residents romanceable?

No. The game has more than 30 residents but only 12 potential romance options.
