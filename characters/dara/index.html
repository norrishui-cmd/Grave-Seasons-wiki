Dara in Grave Seasons

Dara is a confirmed Ashenridge resident who works as player character and farmer and is associated with the player's farm. Dara is the default name used for the customizable player character: an escaped convict who comes to Ashenridge to claim a former cellmate's farm. Players can customize the character's name, appearance, and pronouns.
