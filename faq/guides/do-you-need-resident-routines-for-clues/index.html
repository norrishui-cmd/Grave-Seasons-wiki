Guide FAQ

Do resident routines matter when following clues?

Yes. The developer's journal example shows that knowing a resident's usual locations can help when a clue points to them but their workplace is closed.

What is confirmed

Exact schedules are not published yet, so pre-release guides can document confirmed jobs and associated buildings without inventing hourly routes. Retail testing will add times, seasonal changes, and campaign exceptions.

Why this matters

This matters for walkthrough design because Grave Seasons combines a finite calendar with campaign-dependent clues and resident states. Our guides separate universal mechanics from killer-specific steps, identify the relevant season and survival state, and place direct answers before spoiler details.

What remains outside this answer

Unpublished values, schedules, item names, routes, rewards, campaign identities, and platform behavior are not inferred. This page will add exact steps or tables only after an official update or repeatable test in the retail build provides a concrete answer.

Source and update policy

Current information is checked against the developer interview. Older announcements and cached storefront data are not allowed to override a newer official status. Corrections retain the same canonical URL so search engines and players reach one maintained answer.
