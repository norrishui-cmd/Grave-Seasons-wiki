Character FAQ

Can the same character have different story roles?

Yes. A resident can have different relevance when they are alive, dead, a victim, or the active killer in separate playthroughs.

Quick answer status

  • Answer type: confirmed current information
  • Category: Characters
  • Last reviewed: July 19, 2026
  • Source class: official Steam description

What is confirmed

This is why character pages use state-specific sections rather than presenting one schedule or case role as universal.

Why this matters

Character information is especially spoiler-sensitive because a resident may be an ordinary neighbor, romance option, victim, or killer in different campaigns. Profiles keep stable identity facts separate from campaign outcomes and do not invent gifts or schedules.

What remains outside this answer

Unpublished values, schedules, item names, routes, rewards, campaign identities, and platform behavior are not inferred. This page will add exact steps or tables only after an official update or repeatable test in the retail build provides a concrete answer.

Source and update policy

Current information is checked against the official Steam description. Older announcements and cached storefront data are not allowed to override a newer official status. Corrections retain the same canonical URL so search engines and players reach one maintained answer.
